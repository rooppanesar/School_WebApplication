Additional Programs

​Toy Library (Toys on Rent)

SGTS presents the concept of Toy Library...YES the TOY LIBRARY, Where you can rent the toys for your Little One's. Now, Why should you rent toys,when you can buy them?.The answer to this is exactly the reverse of the question "Why should you buy toys, when you can rent them?". There are a number of benefits in renting vis-à-vis buying most important among them is the fact that renting is far more economical than buying. Moreover, children love variety and get bored with the same toys. And our homes, too, don't have space to stock unlimited toys. That's where renting provides a feasible and affordable solution.

​Activity Center (After School)
